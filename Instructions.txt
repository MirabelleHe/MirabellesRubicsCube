Welcome to Mirabelle's Rubik's Cube!!! 
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

To enter the game go to "forClass3D" folder and press "Mirabelles Rubiks Cube.exe".

First you need to mess all the cubes and than try and bring it back to the original state.

Buttons:
Right Arrow - Move whole cube right
Left Arrow - Move whole cube left
Up Arrow - Move whole cube up
Down Arrow - Move whole cube down
R - Rotate right wall
L - Rotate left wall
U - Rotate up wall
D - Rotate down wall
F - Rotate fron wall
B - Rotate back wall
Space - flip direction, press again to return ro default direction (default = clock wise)
A - Rotate 180 degrees, press again to return to default (default = 90 degrees)

Hope you'll enjoy the game :)
